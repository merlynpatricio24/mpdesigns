About The Project

The app focused on redefining your everyday weather app into a much more clean, neat, and relaxing interface while at the same time providing the users with the relevant information that they need. The app is available as for mobile in both iOS and Android considering that people more often care about the weather when they are about to leave the house.

Objectives

Provide users with the necessary information pertaining to the weather.

Improve the mood of the user through its pleasant aesthetic

Logo Design

I wanted a logo that can cover the entire day which led me to a design with both sun and moon in it. I wanted to apply symmetry in the logo making it easier to use especially in the context of mobile. The stars and sunbeams help distinguish the sun from the moon. Having a a simple white color goes back to the simplicity of the app as well as better contrast with the background.
