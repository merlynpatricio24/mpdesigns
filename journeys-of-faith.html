About The Project

Considering the nature of the business, I was tasked to improve the overall UX/UI of the website catering to its niche market. As a running business, I need to provide a new look for the current website and improve the strengths and address the weaknesses of the current website

The Client

Journeys of Faith is a travel agency in the Philippines specialized in providing pilgrimage tours around the world. It also provides customizable tours.

The Objective

Considering the nature of the business, I was tasked to improve the overall UX/UI of the website catering to its niche market. As a running business, I need to provide a new look for the current website and improve the strengths and address the weaknesses of the current website.

Methods Used

Interviews

Conducted 7 in depth interviews with various users within the target market. Also conducted interviews within members of the team

Card Sorting

A session wherein each team member was to write their own 15 must-haves for the website and grouped them according to similarity.

Personas

User archetypes that have details on the kind of profile that specific type of customer has for deeper understanding of the target market.

User Journey Maps

A flow showing the journey of the user from point A to point B highlighting possible pains and gains with varying degrees.

Heuristics Evaluations

An evaluation on the site’s good and bad case practices grounded on UX/UI design principles.

Thematic Analysis

Using the data set to categories insight into themes providing a better view of the issue at hand.

Overall Key Findings

Based on all the data and information gathered from various sources and tools

Content

Due to lack of relevance, some info needs to be scrapped.

Look and Feel

Needs to be more lively, engaging, and “travel-esque”.

Navigation

Due to confusion, there are some navigational concerns that need to be addressed.

Aesthetic

Users prefer to see more visuals and know what is in store for them.

Getting to the Final Output

How I led to these designs

Evaluation and Analysis

Studied the current situation of the site as well as its competitors.

Low Fidelity Designs

Created wireframes that were easy to alter. Talked with the client present and figured out how to solve the problems found from the research. This process had consistent iterations.

High Fidelity Designs

After several cycles of iterations, the batch of designs were made into its high fidelity state and was further tested.
