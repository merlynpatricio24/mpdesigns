About The Project

The project is about redesigning the website of Webhallen. Webhallen Sverige AB is a Swedish e-commerce company that sells games, movies, software, hardware, consumer electronics and household products through both e-commerce and physical stores. The website has an outdated design and I thought of providing a modern and simplistic redesign to accommodate the new trends of today.

Considering that the company uses the Swedish language, its my first project wherein the entire website is designed in another language. There were several challenges concerning the culture, terminology, and colloquial terms that I had to be aware about. This provided an avenue for me to break out of my comfort zone and to really be immersed in the Swedish vocabulary.

Insights On Current Website

- Too many elements fighting for my attention
- Too much space to accommodate the “chat” feature
- Repetition of information with the nav bar and the side bar
- Unnecessary elements such as “Facebook like”, “Facebook post”, and “Poll Questions”.

- Layout of the website can be improved
- Very text heavy with lack of focus for the eye
- IA can be improved with some elements not being as consistent
