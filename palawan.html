About The Project

The beauty of Palawan is absolutely one of a kind. As one of the places that everyone must have the chance to see, it would be great to have all the important information to be in one place. Additionally, the project aims to showcase the beauty and wonder of Palawan to the world.
As a Filipino, I have a strong connection towards Palawan as it is one of the most treasured places here in the Philippines not just because of its beautiful sites but also because of the people, the culture, and the resources it holds that caters to the people living there. This project is meant to showcase my skills as a designer particularly in terms of UI design. In this non-commercial project, I show how various design principles such as Color Theory, Rule of Thirds, and many more create a beautiful output.

Objectives

- Be able to highlight the beauty of the island as well as the wonderful culture of the people in it.

- Help aid the city by encouraging tourism which is a big contributor to the wealth of the island

- Educate the facts regarding Palawan as well as provide information of the current state of the city
