My Story

My name is Merlyn Anthony Patricio. I am a graduate of the Ateneo de Manila University in the Philippines with a degree in Information Technology and Entrepreneurship. and currently situated in Metro Manila, Philippines. As someone who loves figuring out how things work and how people think, I am always curious about the things around me and am constantly trying to figure out how to make things better and more enjoyable for me and for others. Through UX design, I am able to understand customers on a deeper level and am able to improve the experience that the business provides which will ultimately help the business achieve its goals. I do this because I want to make sure that people make the most of whatever is in front of them by enhancing their experience by getting the full benefit of any product of service.

My Process

STRATEGY

Involves goal setting as well as expectation setting. Inquiry of overall
business strategies, objectives, and member roles.

RESEARCH

Involves use of data acquisition methods such as interviews,
card sorting, surveys, and observations.

ANALYSIS

Using tools to understand the business problem and other
factors through User Personas, Journey Maps,
Sitemapping, Competitive Audit, etc.

DESIGN

Constructing wireframes, mockups, and prototypes grounded
on data. Involves close communication with the business
adopting an Agile Methodology.

BETA TEST

Providing closed beta testing sessions to get user insight.

EVALUATE

Rapid changes to adapt to validated user insight.

LAUNCH

Iterations are still possible if necessary
