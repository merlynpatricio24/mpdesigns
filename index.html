Eden Nature Park Redesign

This project focuses on giving Eden a new fresh redesign to keep up with the modern times. With this new design, I wanted to recreate the feeling of being cozy out in a place of wonder and beauty. I covered different aspects of the park website such as IA, UX/UI, and Branding to tie everything together creating consistency in the experience.

IMDb Website Redesign

If you like watching movies then you probably have encountered IMDb before. One of the great things about IMDb is its amazing amount of content for users but also because of this, the website can get very cluttered with all the information on your screen. This redesign gives you an idea of what it can be.

Weather App Design

With all the various weather app designs out there, I thought that I'd give my take on it as well. The focus of this weather app design is simplicity and minimalism. The overall aesthetics of the design is grounded on consistency as well as functionality. Every element is placed for a specific reason for the benefit of the users wherever they go.

Palawan Tourism Website

Palawan is one of the most visited places in the Philippines and is well known all over the world for its pristine beaches and fine sand. This makes tourism one of the most important factors in the economy of the city. Tourists often rely on information on the web which is why I have made a website design to help bring out the most of this amazing place.

Travel Agency UX Case

Journeys of Faith is a flight agency specializing in pilgrimages and holy trips. They facilitate the entire trip from tickets all the way to lodging in order for the customer not to worry about all of those things and just focus on the experience. As a UX designer, my job was to redesign their entire UX/UI for a better experience and higher conversions.

Grocery App UX Case

Groceries can be frustrating especially when you're in a queue to pay. This app design takes away that frustration and helps you in your grocery shopping by being your all-in-one companion with everything you'll need when you shop. From looking for recipes all the way to knowing the latest discounts, this app has it all.
